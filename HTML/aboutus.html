To contribute in building a sustainable future is our key guiding principle as we do and build better business at Torrent Tissues. We are an eco-friendly company founded on a belief that protecting our environment, especially as the world faces climate change is as important as achieving business success.

Torrent Tissues started operations in 2007 with four paper making machines and four converting machines using post-consumer fiber or waste paper as raw materials to produce eco-products like bathroom tissues, paper napkins, paper towels and slitted jumbo rolls or hard rolls. We also offer premium-grade products using virgin pulp from tree farms to remain adherent to sustainability. Today, Torrent Tissues has a dynamic workforce with 10+ paper making machines and more than 15 converting machines located in the 1000 Sq. Yards complex in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India.

We at Torrent Tissues are commited to deliver 100% recyclable paper tissue products as our highest priority is consumer as well as environment first.
